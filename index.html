Kathmandu

The capital city of Nepal, rich in culture, history, and temples like Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath.

Famous for its artistic heritage, ancient palaces, and the beautiful Patan Durbar Square.

A medieval city known for its well-preserved architecture and rich Newari culture.

A paradise for adventure lovers, offering stunning lake views and access to the Annapurna range.

Chitwan

Home to Chitwan National Park, offering jungle safaris and a chance to see rhinos and tigers.

Lumbini

The birthplace of Lord Buddha, known for its peaceful monasteries and historical significance.

Janakpur

A cultural and religious hub, famous for the Janaki Temple dedicated to Goddess Sita.

Ilam

Known for its scenic tea gardens, lush hills, and breathtaking landscapes.
