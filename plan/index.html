Spring (March-May)

Perfect for trekking and mountain views. Rhododendrons in bloom across hillsides.

Moderate Temperature Clear Skies Festival Season

Autumn (Sept-Nov)

The most popular season. Crystal clear mountain views and stable weather conditions.

Winter (Dec-Feb)

Cold in mountains but perfect for lower altitude treks. Less crowded trails.

Clear Views Less Crowded Lower Prices

Lush green landscapes but rain can obstruct mountain views. Good for rain shadow areas.

Health & Safety

Recommended Vaccinations:

Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis, Rabies

Altitude Sickness:

A concern for high-altitude trekking. Acclimatize properly and recognize symptoms.

Travel Insurance:

Highly recommended, should include emergency evacuation coverage for trekkers.
